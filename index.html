Scan → Learn

Students point their phone camera at the QR code printed in their textbook or shown on screen. The browser opens instantly — no app download needed.

🌐

WebXR Technology

Powered by WebXR Device API and Three.js, 3D models are anchored in real-world space. Students can walk around, tap to explore parts, zoom and rotate.

🧩

Three.js Rendering

High-quality 3D rendering runs entirely in the browser. GLTF/GLB model format ensures small file sizes and great cross-platform performance.

🎓

For Educators

Teachers create topics, upload or link 3D models, and print QR codes. The entire workflow takes under 2 minutes per topic.
